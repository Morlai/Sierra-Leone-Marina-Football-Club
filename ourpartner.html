Empowering Youth Together

SL Marina Football Club does not stand alone. We are proud to collaborate with visionary organizations, local businesses, and community leaders who share our passion for developing the next generation of Sierra Leonean talent. Together, we are building a brighter future.

Official Kit Sponsor

Freetown Sports Apparel

Providing high-quality training kits, match-day jerseys, and equipment to ensure our players look and feel like professionals.

Health & Wellness Partner

Mariners Medical Group

Ensuring the health and safety of our athletes with on-site medical support, physiotherapy, and nutritional guidance programs.

Education Partner

Sierra Leone Youth Fund

Supporting our dual-career pathway by providing scholarships, tutoring, and educational materials for our youth academy players.

Media Partner

SL Sports Network

Amplifying our story and broadcasting our matches to inspire communities across the nation and beyond.

Community Partner

Green Freetown Initiative

Collaborating on community clean-up drives and environmental awareness campaigns alongside our football outreach programs.

Hospitality Partner

Coastal Refreshments Co.

Keeping our players and fans hydrated and energized during intense training sessions and exciting match days.
