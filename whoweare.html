More Than a Club.
We Are a Movement.

Born in the heart of Freetown, SL Marina Football Club was founded on a simple but powerful belief: football has the power to transform lives. We are not just a team that plays on the pitch; we are a family dedicated to nurturing talent, building character, and uplifting our community.

From the dusty streets to the bright lights of the stadium, we provide a safe haven where young athletes can dream big, work hard, and achieve greatness. We are the proud representatives of Sierra Leone's resilient spirit.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a beacon of hope in Sierra Leonean football.

2014

The Foundation

SL Marina FC was officially registered, starting with a small group of passionate youths and a single football on a local pitch in Freetown.

2017

Youth Academy Launch

We established our formal youth development program, introducing structured coaching, education support, and nutritional guidance.

2020

Community Outreach Expansion

Launched major community initiatives, including food drives and health awareness campaigns, solidifying our role beyond the pitch.

Present

Rising to New Heights

Today, we are a recognized force in Sierra Leonean football, continuously producing talent and inspiring the next generation.
