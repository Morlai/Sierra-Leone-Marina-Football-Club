Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about donating? We've got answers.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

SL Marina Football Club is a registered community organization. Donations may be tax-deductible depending on your country's laws. Please consult your tax advisor for specific guidance.

How will I know my donation was received?

You will receive an email confirmation within 24 hours of your donation. For mobile money transfers, please keep your transaction receipt and our team will verify it promptly.

Can I choose which child to sponsor?

While we cannot assign specific children, Champion ($50) and Legend ($100+) donors receive monthly updates about the children their donations are helping, including names, photos, and progress reports.

Is there a minimum donation amount?

No! Every contribution matters. Even $1 can help buy a meal for a child after training. We appreciate donations of any size.

Can I volunteer instead of donating money?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers — coaches, teachers, mentors, and helpers. Visit our Get Involved page to learn more about volunteering opportunities.

How can I donate from outside Sierra Leone?

International donors can use PayPal, bank transfer, or contact us directly at slmarinersfc@gmail.com for alternative arrangements. We accept USD, GBP, and EUR.
