The Heart of SL Marina FC

At SL Marina Football Club, we are driven by more than just the love of the game. We are fueled by a deep commitment to our community, our players, and the future of Sierra Leone. Our foundation is built on clear principles that guide every decision we make, every training session we lead, and every life we touch.

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Freetown through the transformative power of football. We provide a safe, structured environment that fosters athletic excellence, academic support, and character development, ultimately lifting our players and their communities toward a brighter, self-sufficient future.

Our Vision

To be the premier football academy and community organization in Sierra Leone. We envision a future where every child, regardless of their background, has access to world-class coaching, education, and a clear pathway to professional football, using the sport as a catalyst for national pride and social change.

Our Core Values

Excellence: Striving for the highest standards on and off the pitch.
Integrity: Playing fair and leading with honesty.
Community: Uplifting each other and the neighborhoods we serve.
Resilience: Overcoming life's challenges with determination and grace.

What We Stand For

We stand for the inherent potential of every child in Sierra Leone. We believe football is more than a game; it is a vital tool for education, health awareness, and social mobility. We stand against poverty and inequality, offering a beacon of hope and a structured path to success.
