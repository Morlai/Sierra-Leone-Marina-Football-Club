About SL Marina Football Club

Empowering the youth of Freetown through the beautiful game of football, fostering talent, discipline, and community spirit.

Founded with a passion for sports and community development, SL Marina Football Club is more than just a team. We are a family dedicated to nurturing local talent in Sierra Leone, providing a safe and structured environment for young athletes to thrive both on and off the pitch.

  • Professional youth development and football training
  • Community outreach and social empowerment programs
  • Promoting teamwork, discipline, and healthy lifestyles

Our vision is to become a leading football institution in West Africa, producing top-tier athletes who will represent Sierra Leone on the global stage while giving back to their communities.

Our Journey

From a local community team to a recognized football club in Sierra Leone.

Building a Legacy in Freetown

What started as a group of passionate young players gathering at the local pitches has grown into a structured football club with a clear mission. SL Mariners FC was established to keep the youth engaged, away from the streets, and focused on education and sports.

Over the years, we have participated in numerous regional tournaments, built partnerships with local sponsors, and created a support system that helps our players succeed in life, not just in football.

Key Milestones

  • Officially registered as a community football club in Freetown.
  • Launched our youth academy to train players under 15 and under 17.
  • Partnered with local businesses and sponsors to provide kits and equipment.
  • Organized community charity matches to support local causes.

Every day, we continue to write our history with every pass, every goal, and every young life we touch.
