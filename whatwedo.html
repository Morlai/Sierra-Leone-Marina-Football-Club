Transforming Lives Through Football

At SL Marina Football Club, we go far beyond the pitch. We are dedicated to holistic youth development, combining elite football training with education, mentorship, and community empowerment to build the confident leaders of tomorrow in Sierra Leone.

Youth Football Academy

Providing professional, age-grade coaching to nurture raw talent, develop technical skills, and instill tactical intelligence in young athletes.

Education & Mentorship

Football is the hook, education is the goal. We offer tutoring, scholarship support, and life-skills mentoring to ensure dual-career success.

Community Outreach

Organizing neighborhood clean-ups, health awareness campaigns, and inclusive sports days to unite and uplift the Freetown community.

Health & Nutrition

Ensuring our players are physically ready to perform with access to basic medical care, physiotherapy, and nutritious post-training meals.

Competitive Match Days

Entering local and regional leagues to give our youth real-world competitive experience, building resilience and team spirit under pressure.

Talent Pathways

Connecting our most promising players with scouts, higher-level academies, and scholarship opportunities to elevate their careers.

The SL Mariners Pathway

How we guide a young athlete from their first day on the pitch to becoming a confident leader.

1. Discover

Open trials and community scouting identify raw talent and passion in local neighborhoods.

2. Develop

Rigorous training, coupled with academic support, builds well-rounded, disciplined individuals.

3. Compete

Players test their skills in organized leagues, learning teamwork and sportsmanship under pressure.

4. Excel

Graduates move on to higher-level clubs, universities, or become community leaders themselves.
